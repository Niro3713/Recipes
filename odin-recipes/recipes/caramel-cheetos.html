Caramel Cheetos Recipe

These caramel cheetos are the perfect salty-sweet snack.

Caramel cheetos feature cheese and caramel flavors and are irresistible. Read below to find out how to make this dish yourself!

Ingredients

  • 2 (8.5 oz) bags crunchy cheese curls (such as CheetosÂ®)
  • 1 cup light corn syrup
  • 1 cup brown sugar
  • 1 stick (8 oz) butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 250 degrees F (120 degrees C). Line a large rimmed baking sheet with parchment paper.
  2. Pour cheetos into a large bowl.
  3. Combine light corn syrup, brown sugar, and butter in a saucepan over medium high heat. Stir constantly and bring the mixture to a boil. Cook for 1 minute and then pour mixture over the cheetos.
  4. Working quickly, stir to coat cheetos with sugar mixture and pour them out onto the prepared baking sheet. Spread them out into an even layer.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 1 hour, stirring every 15 minutes. Remove from the oven and allow to cool completely. Break any clumps into small pieces. Serve immediately or store in an airtight container.
