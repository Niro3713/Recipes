Coquito Recipe

A Puerto Rican Drink Served During the Holidays!

Coquito is a traditional holiday drink that originated in Puerto Rico. In Spanish, coquito means "little coconut." The beverage is coconut-based and spiked with rum.

Ingredients

  • Evaporated milk
  • Eggs
  • Cream of coconut
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • White rum
  • Water
  • Vanilla
  • Spices

Steps

  1. Cook the evaporated milk and egg yolks in a double boiler until thick.
  2. Transfer the mixture to a blender and blend with the remaining ingredients.
  3. Transfer to glass bottles and refrigerator until cold.
